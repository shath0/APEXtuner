Setup Sheet

Pick a car and hit Generate Values are FM2023 starting points — test on track and trim from there.

APEX generates baseline tunes from the FM2023 physics model. Springs, ride height and dampers are given as a percentage of each car's slider range (they're car-specific); ARB (1–65), dampers (1–20), diff and brakes are absolute. In-game behaviour is always ground truth — treat this as a starting point and iterate. Not affiliated with Turn 10 or Microsoft.
